MY TOP 3 NETFLIX SERIES "THRILLER"

1. Squid Game

Squid Game (Korean: 오징어게임; RR: Ojingeo Geim), South Korean television series is written and directed by Hwang Dong-hyuk. The original Netflix 8 episodes series, starring Lee Jung-jae, Park Hae-soo, Wi Ha-joon and Lee Ji-ha, tells the story of people who risk their life in a mysterious survival game having a prize of ₩45.6 billion. It released on September 17, 2021 worldwide by Netflix in 190 countries

2. Sweet Home

Sweet Home is a South Korean apocalyptic horror streaming television series starring Song Kang, Lee Jin-wook, Lee Si-young and Lee Do-hyun. Based on the Naver webtoon of the same name by Kim Carnby and Hwang Young-chan, which recorded over 2.1 billion net views, the series was released on Netflix on December 18, 2020.

3. Alice In Borderland

Alice in Borderland premiered on Netflix on December 10, 2020, where it was met with positive reviews from viewers and critics for its visuals, cinematography, editing, and use of graphic violence, with some comparing the series to the films Battle Royale (2000) and Cube (1997). Alice in borderland series is adaptation of manga "alice in Borderland".
